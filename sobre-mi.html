I am Evelyn, founder of BellaDona. My passion for hairdressing began more than 15 years ago when I discovered the transformative power that a good haircut has on people's confidence and self-esteem.

Tras formarme en las mejores escuelas de Barcelona y trabajar en reconocidos salones de belleza, decidí abrir mi propio espacio donde poder ofrecer un servicio personalizado y de calidad, centrado en las necesidades específicas de cada cliente. After training at the best schools in Barcelona and working in renowned beauty salons, I decided to open my own space where I could offer a personalized and quality service, focused on the specific needs of each client.
